Who am I?


I'm a Software Engineer from the UK, with over half a decade's experience in developing tooling and games for the Roblox platform!


I have been a member of the Roblox Platform since January 2017, where I started developing games on the platform in December of the same year


I believe I have a vocation for game development and aspire for it to be a full-time career, with computers taking up a good portion of my childhood.


I've mostly worked as a solo programmer on my projects, however I have worked in teams previously and am open to working in teams.


I understand the industry standards, so you can get your projects delivered at the absolute best quality.

Playverse Studio

June 2024 - Current

Playverse Studio is a Roblox game studio with over 1 BILLION total visits.

I have worked on many projects, however none have been released yet due to unforseen circumstances.

My largest project was one named "Presidents!", a rap-battle styled game which involved two candidates arguing for who would be the best president.

Unfortunately, we planned this project around the launch of the Presidential Election in the US, and we missed our deadline - we also had problems with some of the features not working how we really wanted. This caused us to scrap the game and move on.
